GPT-5 vs Claude 4 Opus: The Battle for the Smartest AI Model

M
Maria S. · May 12, 2026 · 8 min read

How We Tested

We ran both models through 50+ standardized tasks across six categories: reasoning, coding, creative writing, document analysis, mathematics, and instruction following. All tests were run blind by three independent evaluators.

Reasoning & Problem Solving

Claude 4 Opus narrowly edges out GPT-5 on complex reasoning tasks, particularly those requiring multi-step logic and the ability to maintain consistent reasoning across long contexts. GPT-5 closes the gap on shorter reasoning challenges.

💡
Key finding: For tasks requiring reasoning over 50,000+ tokens (large documents, codebases), Claude 4 Opus wins decisively due to its superior context retention.

Coding Quality

Both models write excellent code. Claude 4 Opus produces slightly more maintainable code with better comments. GPT-5 is faster and better at generating boilerplate rapidly. For complex architecture decisions, Claude wins. For speed, GPT-5.

Creative Writing

GPT-5 is more creatively surprising and takes more interesting narrative risks. Claude 4 Opus writes more polished, technically correct prose. For marketing copy: Claude wins. For fiction and creative ideation: GPT-5.

Price Comparison

Both Claude 4 Opus Pro and ChatGPT Plus are priced at $20/month. At the API level, pricing is comparable — check the latest rates on each company's pricing page as these update frequently.

Our Verdict

For most users, both models are exceptional. If you work with documents, code review, or complex analysis: Claude 4 Opus. If you want the best creative output and widest ecosystem: GPT-5.
