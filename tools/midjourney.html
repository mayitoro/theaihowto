What it can do

Key Features

Industry-leading image quality

Midjourney V7 produces images with unprecedented realism and artistic quality. Fine details like hands, faces, and text (in supported modes) are handled more accurately than any competing model. The default output consistently passes for professional photography or commissioned illustration.

Style references (--sref)

Use any image as a style reference with --sref [URL]. Midjourney matches the color palette, artistic style, and compositional approach of the reference image — essential for maintaining brand consistency across a series of images.

Character consistency (--cref)

Use --cref [URL] to maintain the same character or person across multiple generations. Create a character once and generate them in different scenes, poses, and situations while keeping their appearance consistent — a major breakthrough for storytelling and marketing.

Aspect ratio control

Generate images in any aspect ratio: --ar 16:9 (landscape/video thumbnail), --ar 9:16 (portrait/mobile), --ar 1:1 (square/social media), --ar 4:5 (Instagram portrait), --ar 3:2 (photography standard). Getting the aspect ratio right is essential for professional use.

Vary and edit tools

After generating an image, use Vary (Subtle) or Vary (Strong) to explore similar variations. Use the web-based editor to make targeted changes: swap backgrounds, change clothing, add elements — without regenerating the entire image from scratch.

Fast and Relax modes

Fast mode uses dedicated GPU time for near-instant generation (seconds). Relax mode is free of charge against your GPU time allocation but queues generation when servers are busy. Standard and Pro plans include significant Fast GPU time plus unlimited Relax mode.

Step by step

How to get started

1

Subscribe at midjourney.com

Go to midjourney.com and click Sign In → Authorize with Discord. Midjourney requires a Discord account. Choose a plan — Basic ($10/mo) for 200 images/month, Standard ($30/mo) for unlimited Relax mode. There is no free trial in 2026.

2

Access via web or Discord

You can generate images through the midjourney.com web interface (recommended for beginners) or in the Midjourney Discord server. The web interface is cleaner and easier to manage your gallery. Discord is preferred by power users for its speed.

3

Write your first prompt

In the Imagine box, describe your image. A strong prompt has 4 parts: Subject ('a golden retriever'), Context ('running on a beach at sunset'), Style ('Canon EOS R5, f/2.8, bokeh, National Geographic photo'), Parameters ('--ar 16:9 --v 7 --style raw'). Be specific — vague prompts get mediocre results.

4

Use parameters for control

Essential parameters: --ar [ratio] sets aspect ratio. --v 7 uses the latest model. --style raw reduces Midjourney's artistic interpretation for more photorealistic results. --q 2 uses maximum quality (slower). --no [thing] removes unwanted elements. --chaos [0-100] adds variation.

5

Iterate with variations

After generation, you see 4 options (U1-U4 to upscale, V1-V4 for variations). Click U1 to upscale your favorite. Click V2 to create 4 variations of the second image. Use the web editor to make targeted adjustments. Save images by clicking Download in the web gallery.

6

Use style and character references

For consistent style: 'minimalist product photo --sref [URL of a photo you like]'. For consistent characters: 'a superhero in a city --cref [URL of your character image]'. These two parameters transform Midjourney from a random generator into a consistent production tool.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free version of Midjourney?▾
No. Midjourney removed its free trial in mid-2023. All plans require a paid subscription starting at $10/month. If you want free AI image generation, try: DALL·E 3 (via ChatGPT free), Microsoft Copilot (DALL·E 3 free with daily boosts), or Adobe Firefly (free tier available).
What is the difference between --style raw and default style?▾
Default Midjourney applies strong artistic processing — it makes images look more 'Midjourney-like' with vibrant colors and stylized aesthetics. --style raw reduces this processing for results closer to photorealistic photography. For product photography, portraits, and realistic scenes: use --style raw. For artistic illustrations: use default.
Can I use Midjourney images commercially?▾
Yes, with conditions. Basic plan users ($10/mo) may use images commercially if their annual company revenue is under $1M. Standard and Pro plan users have full commercial rights. Enterprise plans are available for larger companies. Always read the updated terms at midjourney.com/docs/terms.
How do --sref and --cref work?▾
--sref [image URL] tells Midjourney to match the visual style of the referenced image. --cref [image URL] tells it to maintain the same character or person. You can combine both: generate a specific character (--cref) in a specific artistic style (--sref). The strength can be adjusted with --sref [URL] --sw 50 (0-100 scale).
What is Stealth Mode?▾
Available on Pro ($60/mo) and Mega ($120/mo) plans. Normally, all generated images are visible in your public gallery at midjourney.com and in Discord. Stealth Mode keeps your generations private — they don't appear in the public gallery. Essential for commercial projects where you don't want to reveal concepts to competitors.
