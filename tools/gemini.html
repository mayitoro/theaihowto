Gemini

★★★★★ 4.5/5 Freemium
Google's AI with real-time search and 1M token context

Gemini is Google's most powerful AI model, with native real-time web search, a 1-million-token context window, and deep integration with Google Workspace — Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Drive, and Meet.

What it can do

Key Features

Real-time web search built in

Unlike ChatGPT which decides when to search, Gemini searches the web by default for most queries, always returning up-to-date answers with source citations. This makes it the most reliable for questions about current events, prices, and recent developments.

1 million token context window

Gemini 1.5 Pro offers the longest context window available — 1 million tokens, equivalent to roughly 700,000 words or about 1,500 pages. This makes it uniquely powerful for analyzing entire codebases, long legal documents, or full research papers.

Native Google Workspace integration

Gemini is embedded directly in Gmail (summarize emails, draft replies), Google Docs (generate and edit text), Google Sheets (create formulas, analyze data), Google Slides (create presentations), and Google Meet (summarize meetings in real-time).

Imagen 3 image generation

Gemini Advanced includes access to Imagen 3, Google's photorealistic image generation model. Produces high-quality images with accurate text rendering — one of the few models that can reliably place readable text in generated images.

Multimodal understanding

Natively understands text, images, audio, video, and code in a single request. Ask Gemini to analyze a video, describe an image in detail, transcribe audio, or explain code — without switching tools.

Gemini in Google Search

AI Overviews in Google Search are powered by Gemini. When you search on Google, Gemini synthesizes information from multiple sources and presents a concise answer at the top of results.

Step by step

How to get started

1

Access Gemini at gemini.google.com

Go to gemini.google.com or download the Gemini app. Sign in with your existing Google account — no new account needed. If you already use Gmail, you already have access.

2

Try a research query first

Gemini shines on research tasks. Try: 'What are the most important AI developments in the last 6 months, with sources?' Notice how Gemini automatically cites its sources — this is one of its biggest advantages over ChatGPT's default behavior.

3

Use Gemini inside Gmail

Open Gmail on desktop → look for the Gemini icon (sparkle ✦) in the compose window or sidebar. Click it and ask: 'Summarize the last 5 emails from John' or 'Draft a polite decline to this meeting invitation.'

4

Use Gemini in Google Docs

Open a Google Doc → click Help me write (or the Gemini icon in the toolbar). You can generate content, rewrite paragraphs, summarize the document, or ask questions about it. This is faster than copy-pasting to a separate chat.

5

Try multimodal inputs

Upload an image and ask: 'What does this diagram show? List the 5 key components.' Or paste a YouTube URL and ask: 'Summarize the main points of this video in bullet points.' Gemini handles mixed inputs naturally.

6

Upgrade to Gemini Advanced

Google One AI Premium ($19.99/mo) unlocks Gemini Ultra (the most powerful model), Imagen 3 image generation, longer Workspace integration, and 2TB of Google storage. If you already pay for Google One storage, this is excellent value.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gemini free to use?▾
Yes. Gemini is completely free with any Google account at gemini.google.com. The free version uses Gemini Flash and includes real-time web search, image understanding, and basic Workspace integration. Gemini Advanced ($19.99/mo via Google One AI Premium) unlocks the Ultra model, Imagen 3, and deeper Workspace features.
What is the difference between Gemini Flash and Gemini Ultra?▾
Gemini Flash (free) is fast and capable for most everyday tasks. Gemini Ultra (Advanced plan) is significantly more powerful for complex reasoning, longer documents, creative tasks, and professional use cases. Think of Flash as the free daily driver and Ultra as the serious work tool.
Can Gemini see my emails?▾
Only if you give permission. In Gmail, Gemini can access your emails when you explicitly ask it to — for example 'Summarize my unread emails from today.' You can revoke this access at any time in your Google Account settings under Data & Privacy.
How does Gemini compare to ChatGPT?▾
For research and factual questions, Gemini is often better — it always searches the web with sources. For creative writing, code quality, and complex reasoning, ChatGPT and Claude have an edge. For anyone already in Google Workspace, Gemini's integration advantages are significant.
What is Google's AI Overview in Search?▾
When you search on Google, AI Overviews (powered by Gemini) appear at the top of results for complex queries. They synthesize information from multiple sources into a direct answer. This is Gemini's most widely-used deployment — it reaches billions of users.
