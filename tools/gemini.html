Gemini: Google's multimodal AI with real-time search

Gemini is Google's flagship AI assistant, natively integrated with Search, Gmail, Docs, and the entire Google Workspace. It features a 1 million token context window and built-in real-time web access.

Try Gemini → See how to use it
Key features

What Gemini can do

🔍

Native real-time search

Unlike other AI models, Gemini has web search built in by default — not as a plugin. It always cites sources and pulls live data.

🗂️

Google Workspace integration

Works directly inside Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Meet. Summarize emails, draft documents, and analyze spreadsheets without leaving Google apps.

📊

1 million token context

The largest context window of any major AI model. Process entire codebases, legal documents, or book-length research in one go.

🎨

Imagen 3 image generation

Generate photorealistic images powered by Google's Imagen 3 model, available on Gemini Advanced plans.

🎙️

Multimodal understanding

Analyze images, audio, video, and text together. Ask questions about photos, transcribe audio, or describe what's happening in a video.

⚡

Gemini 2.0 Flash

Ultra-fast responses optimized for speed while maintaining high quality. Available on the free tier.

Step-by-step

How to get started with Gemini

1

Access Gemini

Go to gemini.google.com or open the Gemini app. Sign in with any Google account — no setup needed if you already use Gmail.

2

Try Google Workspace

In Gmail, look for the Gemini button (✦) in the sidebar. Click it to summarize emails, draft replies, or ask questions about your inbox.

3

Use in Google Docs

Open any Google Doc → click Help me write. Gemini will generate, rewrite, or summarize content based on your instructions.

4

Upgrade to Advanced

Google One AI Premium ($19.99/mo) gives you Gemini 2.0 Ultra, the most capable model, plus 2TB storage and Imagen 3 image generation.

5

Try Gemini API

Developers can access Gemini via Google AI Studio (free) or Vertex AI. The API supports the full model family from Flash to Ultra.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Gemini free?▾
Yes. Gemini is free with any Google account and includes real-time web access, image understanding, and Gemini 2.0 Flash. Gemini Advanced ($19.99/mo) gives you the Ultra model, Imagen 3, and deeper Workspace integration.
What's the difference between Gemini and Bard?▾
Bard was Google's previous AI assistant, retired in 2024. Gemini replaced it entirely and is significantly more capable with multimodal features, better reasoning, and Google Workspace integration.
Can Gemini access my Gmail?▾
Yes, with permission. Gemini can read, summarize, and help you respond to emails in Gmail. You control what data it can access in your Google account settings.
How does Gemini compare to ChatGPT for coding?▾
ChatGPT and Claude generally produce better code. Gemini is strong at code but excels more in research tasks that require real-time information. For pure coding, Claude Sonnet is the top choice.
