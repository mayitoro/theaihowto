What it can do

Key Features

Full songs in under 30 seconds

Suno generates complete, production-ready songs — typically 1:30 to 2:00 in length — with a single text prompt. The output includes realistic AI vocals, melody, chord progressions, instrumentation, drums, and a proper song structure (verse, chorus, bridge, outro).

Custom mode for full control

Custom mode separates lyrics, style description, and title into individual fields. Write your own lyrics and specify the style independently: 'upbeat indie rock, female vocalist, guitar-driven, 120 BPM.' This gives dramatically more control than the automatic mode.

Extend and continue songs

Any generated track can be extended. Click the menu → Extend → choose whether to add to the beginning or end. Specify what should happen next: 'instrumental bridge, build back into the chorus.' Build complete full-length songs section by section.

Remix and cover modes

Create a remix of any public Suno track or use an existing song as a style reference. The remix mode lets you change the genre, tempo, and arrangement while keeping the melodic structure — useful for creating multiple versions of the same song.

Inpaint for targeted edits

Inpaint lets you replace specific segments of a generated song without changing the rest. Select a 2-second section of vocals you don't like and regenerate just that segment. Essential for fixing specific timing, pronunciation, or melodic issues in otherwise good tracks.

Commercial MP3 downloads

All paid plan users can download generated tracks as high-quality MP3 files with commercial licensing. Use them for YouTube videos, podcasts, ads, games, apps, and client projects. Files include metadata with the generation prompt embedded.

Step by step

How to get started

1

Create your free account at suno.com

Go to suno.com and sign up with your email, Google, or Discord account. Free accounts get 50 credits per day — each song costs 5 credits, giving you approximately 10 songs per day for free. Credits reset daily at midnight UTC.

2

Try Auto mode first

Click Create and make sure you are in Auto (not Custom) mode. Type a single description: 'A melancholic indie folk song about leaving your hometown, female vocalist, acoustic guitar and piano, mid-tempo.' Click Create. Two variations are generated simultaneously — listen to both and pick your favorite.

3

Switch to Custom mode for control

Click Custom to unlock separate lyrics and style fields. Style field: be specific about genre, subgenre, vocal style, instrumentation, and tempo. Example: 'trap beat, 808 bass, hi-hats, melodic auto-tuned male vocals, dark and moody, 140 BPM'. Lyrics field: write actual song lyrics or use [Verse], [Chorus], [Bridge] markers for structure.

4

Use song structure markers

In Custom mode lyrics, use these markers to define song structure: [Intro], [Verse 1], [Pre-Chorus], [Chorus], [Verse 2], [Bridge], [Outro], [Instrumental]. Also use [male vocal], [female vocal], [spoken word], [whisper] to control vocal style. Markers significantly improve structural coherence.

5

Extend to build a complete song

Generated tracks are ~1:30. To make a full song: click the three dots (⋯) → Extend. In the Extend dialog, describe what should come next: 'Second verse then build into final chorus with more energy, add electric guitar'. Repeat the process to add a bridge, outro, etc. until the song is the length you want.

6

Download and use your tracks

Once satisfied, click the Download icon to save as MP3. Pro users get high-quality downloads with commercial licensing. Add the track to video editing software, embed in websites, use in social media, or upload to music distribution platforms. Always check Suno's commercial terms for your specific use case.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Suno-generated songs commercially?▾
Free tier songs (50 credits/day) are for personal, non-commercial use only. Pro plan ($10/mo) and Premier plan ($30/mo) include commercial licensing for most business uses — YouTube content, podcasts, apps, games, and client work. Enterprise licensing is available for large-scale commercial projects. Read the terms at suno.com/terms before commercial use.
How many songs can I make for free per day?▾
Free accounts receive 50 credits per day, and each song generation costs 5 credits. This gives you approximately 10 full songs per day for free. Credits reset daily at midnight UTC. You can generate two variations simultaneously (10 credits total) to compare options.
How do I get better results from Suno?▾
Three key techniques: 1) Use Custom mode instead of Auto — separate lyrics and style give much more control. 2) Be specific about style — include genre, subgenre, vocal type, key instruments, and tempo. 3) Use structure markers ([Verse 1], [Chorus], [Bridge]) in your lyrics to improve structural coherence. Vague prompts produce generic results.
Can I upload my own melody or recording?▾
Suno v4 introduced a feature to upload audio clips as style references — you can upload a melody or groove and ask Suno to generate a song with a similar feel. This is not the same as fine-grained control over specific notes, but it significantly improves style matching compared to text descriptions alone.
How does Suno compare to Udio?▾
Suno and Udio (suno.com vs udio.com) are the two leading AI music generators. Suno is generally considered more consistent and easier to use. Udio produces slightly more natural-sounding vocals in some genres but is less predictable. Many professionals use both: Suno for reliability, Udio for experimentation.
